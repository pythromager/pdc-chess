PDC Rules

🎯 Objective

Checkmate the opponent's king, or put them in a position where they cannot satisfy their "musts".

♙ Pawn

  • Can only capture other pawns
  • Can also capture knights in the river (rows 4-5, files c-f)
  • Normal movement otherwise

♖ Rook

  • MUST capture if any piece is within 3 squares
  • Can only capture within 3 squares range
  • Can move any distance when not capturing

♘ Knight

  • Can only capture pieces that are giving check
  • Normal movement otherwise

♗ Bishop

  • MUST give check if able to do so
  • Cannot capture (only moves to empty squares)

♕ Queen

  • MUST capture if a non-knight piece is in range
  • Cannot capture knights

♔ King

  • MUST move when in check (cannot block or capture checker)
  • Cannot capture when in check
  • Normal movement otherwise

🌊 The River

Squares c4-f4 and c5-f5 (highlighted in blue). Pawns can capture knights here.

⚡ Musts

When you have a "must" (forced move), you MUST play it unless you can deliver checkmate in 1 move.

📊 Space Rule (15-move)

If 15 moves pass without a capture, the player with more "space" (pieces in opponent's half + king attacks) wins.

🏆 Winning

  • Checkmate
  • Opponent has 2+ musts they can't both satisfy
  • Opponent in check + has another must
  • Time out
  • Resignation
