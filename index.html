PDC Rules

🎯 Objective

Checkmate or put opponent in impossible must situation.

♙ Pawn

  • Captures pawns only
  • Can capture knights in the river (c4-f5)

♖ Rook

  • MUST capture if piece within 3 squares
  • Range limited to 3 squares for captures

♘ Knight

  • Can only capture pieces giving check

♗ Bishop

  • MUST give check if possible
  • Cannot capture

♕ Queen

  • MUST capture non-knights if able
  • Cannot capture knights

♔ King

  • MUST move when in check
  • Cannot capture when in check

🌊 River (c4-f5)

Blue squares where pawns can capture knights.

⚡ Must Override

You can ignore a must ONLY if you deliver checkmate in 1.

📊 15-Move Rule

After 15 moves without capture, player with more space wins.
